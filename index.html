Decriminalizing Prostitution

A free society allows individuals to make choices about their own lives, especially when those choices involve voluntary exchange. Prostitution is one such case.

Criminalization forces the practice underground, increasing danger and reducing safety. Decriminalization would improve conditions and allow individuals to act in their own interest.

Legalizing Kidney Sales

Individuals should have authority over their own bodies. Preventing kidney sales limits personal freedom and economic opportunity.

A legal system would reduce black markets and allow safe, regulated transactions that benefit both parties.

Legalizing Weed Sales

The sale of cannabis is a voluntary exchange. Banning it creates illegal markets instead of eliminating demand.

Legalization would support business growth, reduce crime, and respect personal choice.
